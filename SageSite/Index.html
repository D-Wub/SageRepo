--- Edit Copy Share Avatar Kimi Here is a complete, end-to-end starter kit that gives you: One public “link-in-bio” page (yourname.com) One private dashboard (yourname.com/admin) One-click, simultaneous upload of a video (+ title, description, hashtags, thumbnail) to YouTube, TikTok, Instagram (Reels), Facebook, Twitter, Twitch and LinkedIn (plus any custom site that accepts POST or SFTP). 100 % open-source, no SaaS lock-in. Deploy free on Vercel + PlanetScale in ≈15 min. The stack is Next.js 14 (App Router) + tRPC + Prisma + NextAuth + shadcn/ui + React-Hook-Form + Tailwind. All sensitive tokens are stored encrypted at rest (AES-256-GCM) and never hit the browser.
